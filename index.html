Frequently Asked Questions About IQ Test

What is an IQ test and how does it work?

IQ (Intelligence Quotient) is a measurement of your intelligence expressed in a number. Our free IQ test measures your cognitive abilities through carefully designed questions covering logical reasoning, numerical ability, spatial intelligence, and verbal comprehension. The average IQ score is 100 by definition.

How can I get my IQ score for free?

Getting your IQ score is easy with our free online IQ test. Simply click the "Start Free IQ Test" button above, answer 10 carefully designed questions, and you'll receive your instant IQ score with detailed analysis. No registration or email required.

What is considered a good IQ score?

The average IQ score is 100. Most people score between 85 and 115. Here's the IQ scale breakdown:

  • Above 130 - Genius level
  • 120-129 - Superior intelligence
  • 110-119 - High average
  • 90-109 - Average
  • Below 70 - Below average

Can I practice IQ tests to improve my score?

Yes, practicing IQ tests can help you become familiar with question patterns and improve your performance. While it may not increase your innate intelligence, regular practice can help you achieve a higher score on job assessments and similar tests.

What types of questions are on an IQ test?

Our free IQ test includes questions measuring:

  • Numerical reasoning - Number sequences and patterns
  • Logical reasoning - Abstract thinking and problem-solving
  • Spatial intelligence - Visual pattern recognition
  • Verbal intelligence - Word analogies and comprehension

Is this IQ test accurate and certified?

Our IQ test is designed based on established psychological frameworks including Raven's Progressive Matrices and Wechsler Adult Intelligence Scale (WAIS) principles. While no online test can replace a professional psychological evaluation, our test provides a reliable estimate of your cognitive abilities.
